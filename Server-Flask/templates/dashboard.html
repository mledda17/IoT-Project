How to use the Dashboard for Planty

Hey {% if current_user.is_authenticated %} {{ current_user.username }}!{% endif %}!

This is the dashboard where you can monitor your smart pot. From the navigation bar here to the left you can access to the list of your smart pots. You will find a blue button that allows you to register a new smart pot. To register the smart pot you need to give a name to it, choose the location, the plant and the desired parameters. Also, you can modify the parameters later if you change idea and you can unregister smart pots.

You can see the statistics of the measurements, the log of watering cycles and the ideal parameters suggested by the application. The plant is irrigated based on the weather forecast, thus if rain is expected for a day, the irrigation will be skipped. You can also require a "Terrain Scoring Parameters" which gives you a qualitative score regarding the health state of the plant.
